Enzyme Puzzle

A hands on way of teaching biology and enzymes to kids with visual impairments. It is a puzzle, with pieces that represent enzymes, substrate, and products. With instructions, the student will learn to fit labeled pieces into certain spots, and therefore can picture how the enzyme cycle works.
